9. Sources and Key Takeaway

Key Takeaway

The Gundam Plastic Model Kit, short GunPla, is not just a toy but the product of a complex manufacturing process, marketing strategy, and cultural phenomenon that is still evolving. To continue to grow as an art and as a hobby, GunPla must to address its environmental impact and continue to innovate.
