Summary and final thoughts

9. Sources and Key Takeaways

9.1 Key Takeaways

  • SB-13 is HIPS. FTIR top matches were "Polystyrene High Impact (Butyl)", "Bunatex", and a polystyrene–ethylene–butylene block copolymer. The aromatic ring modes near 1600 cm⁻¹ and the strong 2924/2846 cm⁻¹ aliphatic doublet confirm a styrene matrix with an elastomer second phase.
  • PC7 is LLDPE with paraffin. FTIR top matches were "Polyethylene Hostalen GM 7040", "Polyethylene linear low density", and "Paraffin". The 2914/2847 cm⁻¹ stretches, the 1462 cm⁻¹ scissoring band, and the 720–730 cm⁻¹ rocking doublet are diagnostic of polyethylene.
  • The pairing is engineering, not coincidence. Stiff amorphous HIPS for armor + compliant semicrystalline LLDPE for joints is the same paired-stiffness/compliance strategy used in higher-end mechanical assemblies.
  • Multi-color System Injection drives the no-paint promise. Bandai's up-to-four-color injection machines decouple aesthetic color from post-processing.
  • Closed-loop recycling exists at modest scale. Bandai's Gunpla Recycling Project collected ≈72 t over fiscal 2021–2023; collection is growing at ≈2×/year.
